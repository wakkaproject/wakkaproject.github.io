About WAKKA

WAKKA is a digital NFT project inspired by five rescued cats — Mee, Kuro, Kikki, Miku, and Wakka. The name "WAKKA" means "circle" in Japanese, symbolizing connection, love, and the cycle of care. Through this project, we aim to preserve their stories and share their charm with the world.

Meet Our Cats

Mee

A quiet and affectionate girl with rare hachiware markings. She prefers humans and enjoys peaceful naps together.

Kuro

A sleek black cat with sleepy eyes. A lone wolf type—grumpy sometimes, but deeply attached to people.

Kikki

Fluffy, friendly, and athletic. Kikki is loved by both cats and humans for his kind and balanced personality.

Miku

Playful and round-eyed, Miku loves to socialize with cats and always brings joy to the room.

Wakka

A large white cat with a human-like presence. Calm yet caring, he even mediates fights between other cats.

FAQ / Roadmap

Q: What inspired you to start the WAKKA project?

A: My rescued cats became my family. Each one is unique. I wanted to preserve their stories digitally and share their charm with the world.

Q: Why NFTs?

A: Because NFTs last forever, and it's the best way to share their charm globally.

Q: How do you care for their health?

A: WAKKA and KURO are dealing with urinary stones and on special food. We act fast when any cat is unwell. NFT sales support their wellbeing.

Q: What's your future goal?

A: I want my cats to make people around the world smile. If WAKKA becomes well-loved, I hope to launch a WAKKA meme coin.

Q: Final message?

A: My cats are sweet and full of personality. Please enjoy watching their adventures!
