About WAKKA

WAKKA is a growing NFT project that celebrates the charm of cats and the bonds we share with them.
Named after the Japanese word for "circle" or "ring," WAKKA symbolizes connection, warmth, and continuity.

Our first collection, WAKKA Cats, features 101 unique feline NFTs living on the Polygon blockchain.
These NFTs are inspired by our five beloved rescue cats — Mee, Kuro, Kiki, Miku, and Waka — whose everyday joy and resilience sparked this project.

Proceeds will go toward supporting their lives and happiness, and we hope each NFT creates a small ring of joy that spreads outward — just like a wakka.
