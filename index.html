FAQ / Roadmap

Q: What inspired you to start the WAKKA project?

A: My rescued cats became my family. Each has a unique personality. I wanted to preserve their stories digitally and share their charm with the world.

Q: Why did you choose NFTs as the format?

A: NFTs last forever. It's the best way to tell their stories globally.

Q: How do you care for their health?

A: WAKKA and KURO are currently dealing with urinary stones and eating special food. If any cat feels unwell, we go to the vet immediately. NFT earnings support their health and happiness.

Q: What's your vision for the future?

A: I want my cats to make people happy around the world. If WAKKA becomes loved globally, I hope to create a WAKKA meme coin someday.

Q: Anything you’d like to say to visitors?

A: My cats are full of charm and mischief. Please enjoy watching them shine!
